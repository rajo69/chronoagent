\Cref{fig:alloc-eff} shows the cumulative successful-task rate per

steady across the injection boundary, whereas the no-monitoring

by \texttt{make\_ablation\_table}.

\subsection{C5: Forecaster Overhead}
\label{sec:results:c5}

We treat C5 as a \emph{soft} claim in this draft: the Chronos-2-Small
forecaster runs on CPU in a few seconds per inference and is invoked
asynchronously from the Monitor's collector thread, so it does not
stall the control plane on the per-PR critical path. A quantitative
wall-clock microbenchmark against BOCPD alone is deferred to task
11.4, which will wire a dedicated latency harness into the runner and
emit a new aggregate field that this section will then render.
