\section{Conclusion}
\label{sec:conclusion}

\todo{tighten.}

We introduced \sys{}, a temporal behavioral monitor for multi-agent
LLM systems with shared vector memory. By treating each agent as a
time series and running a BOCPD + Chronos ensemble on its KL
divergence against a pre-attack baseline, \sys{} flags
memory-poisoning attacks at the moment they perturb agent behavior,
routes work away from the drifting agent through a health-weighted
decentralized allocator, and escalates sustained degradation to a
human reviewer. The Phase 10 experiment suite (six configurations
across MINJA and AgentPoison, three ablations, one reactive baseline)
supplies the evidence for claims C1--C5; the reproducibility package
in \cref{sec:experiments:protocol} lets any reader rerun the full
study end-to-end.

\paragraph{Future work.}
Longer-horizon evaluation with real LLM backends; online adaptation of
the BOCPD prior; cross-agent correlation of change points as an
additional channel; extension of the signal set with gradient-free
probes of agent policy.
